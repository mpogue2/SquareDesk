Cross Chain Thru

From eight chain thru: All Right Pull By, then the ends Courtesy Turn while the centers Left Hand Star ½ to end in another eight chain thru formation. The star ends as though the centers had done a Cross Trail Thru.

Note: By definition, "Cross Chain Thru and Roll" is the same as Cross Chain and Roll.
