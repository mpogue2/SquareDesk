Flip the Line ¼, ½, ¾, Full

From waves only: Centers Run and all any-shoulder Tag the Line the given fraction (¼, ½, ¾, Full). Any-shoulder means: From right-hand waves, use a right shoulder pass; from left-hand waves use a left shoulder pass.
