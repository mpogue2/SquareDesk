Zing

From a box circulate or tandem couples: Leaders Zoom but only turn 3/4 (270°) as they go (similar to Quarter Out and Run). Trailers Circulate and Quarter In.

NOTE: Zing is also proper from various 3 & 1 boxes, T-Bone boxes, and from an isolated Tandem.
