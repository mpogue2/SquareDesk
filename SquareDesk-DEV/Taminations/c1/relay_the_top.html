Relay the Top

From parallel waves: All Swing. The centers Arm Turn 3/4 while the ends ½ Circulate. The star Turns 1/4, while the others Trade. The center four dancers in the 6-person wave now Cast Off 3/4, while the others move up (as in Hourglass Circulate). Ends in parallel waves.
