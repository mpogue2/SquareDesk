Cross Your Neighbor

From a Box Circulate: the Trailers Cross Extend and Arm Turn 3/4 while the Leaders do your part of Follow Your Neighbor. Cross Your Neighbor cannot be fractionalized.

From a Single 1/4 Tag: all dancers do the Trailers part (Cross Extend and Arm Turn 3/4).
