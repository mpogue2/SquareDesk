Scoot and Weave

From right-(left-)hand box circulate only: Scoot Back. Those facing in Left (Right) Touch ¼ with each other, while those facing out Quarter Right (Left). Ends in a wave.

From single quarter tag: Extend, Trade, and then Weave the same way you would from box circulate.

Timing: 10
