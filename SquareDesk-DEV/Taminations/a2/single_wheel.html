Single Wheel

From a couple or mini-wave only: With each dancer taking the part of an entire couple, do a Wheel and Deal. This call cannot be fractionalized.

For teaching: You can think of this as a Hinge and Roll. However, this call has only one part—it cannot be fractionalized.

Timing: 4
