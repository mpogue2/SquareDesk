Running Bear

Here is the sequence starting with the heads. Typically heads start twice and sides start twice.

  1. Four ladies chain
  2. Four ladies chain
  3. Rollaway
  4. Heads star thru
  5. Dive thru
  6. (Centers) Pass thru
  7. Dosado
  8. Circle Four Half
  9. Dive thru
  10. Centers square thru while others divide and star thru
  11. Dosado
  12. Circle four half
  13. Dive thru
  14. (Centers) Box the gnat and Pull thru
    Then swing your corner, promenade.
