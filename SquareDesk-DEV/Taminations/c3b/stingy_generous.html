Stingy | Generous anything

Stingy Do the anything call, but subtract a 1/4 turn from the first Arm Turn of the call.

Generous Do the anything call, but add a 1/4 turn from the first Arm Turn of the call.

For the purposes of Stingy and Generous, an Arm Turn is limited to the following movements as defined in the anything call:

  • Arm Turn.
  • Hinge from a Mini-Wave.
  • Trade from a Mini-Wave.
  • Cast Off 3/4 from a Mini-Wave.
  • Star turn, when opposite dancers are in a Mini-Wave
