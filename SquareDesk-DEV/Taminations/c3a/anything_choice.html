<anything> Choice

Parts: 2
From any applicable formation: Do the <anything> call followed by the two parts of Choice (Center Line Lockit as Others Trade; Center Line Hinge as Others Step and Fold). Usually, to make clear that the <anything> call is to be treated as part of the larger call, it will be spoken with the suffix "er’s", e.g., "Spin the Topper’s Choice".
