3/4 the Deucey

Starting formation - Parallel Ocean Waves.

Arm Turn 3/4; Centers Cast Off 3/4 as the Lead End Circulate as the Trailing End 1/2 Circulate; Center Star turn 3/4; those who meet (Center Wave) Cast Off 3/4 as lonesome Center move up (Phantom Hourglass Circulate) to become the End of a Wave. Ends in Parallel Waves.
