Something New

From Columns: #1 and #2 dancers Couple Up as #3 dancers 1/2 Circulate and U-Turn Back as #4 dancers Circulate and Veer Out. Ends in a 1/4 Tag.

Alternative definition: First Two Couple Up as the Last Two Circulate & the Leader inwardly (Right or Left) Roll To A Wave.
