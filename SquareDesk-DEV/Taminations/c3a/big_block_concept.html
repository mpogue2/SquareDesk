Big Block <anything>

CONCEPT

From Interlocked Blocks, Butterfly, “O”, or other applicable formations in a 4x4 matrix (e.g., Leaders Press Ahead from Two-Faced Lines): Execute the given call working in distorted Parallel Lines, and ending with each dancer occupying one of the footprints of the original formation.
