Cross Counter

Cross Counter

Parts: 3

From a 3/4 Tag or other applicable formations: Centers Trade The Wave, Cast Off 3/4 and Counter Rotate 1/4 as the Outsides Cross Cast Back, Touch 1/2, and Step & Fold. A R-H 3/4 Tag ends in Parallel L-H Two-Faced Lines; a L-H 3/4 Tag. Ends in Parallel L-H Waves.

Scoot and Cross Counter

From a 1/4 Tag: Scoot Back, then the three parts of Cross Counter. Ends in Parallel Lines.

Left Scoot and Cross Counter

From a L-H 1/4 Tag only: as above, but the outsides Cross Cast Back, Left Touch 1/2, and Step and Fold.
