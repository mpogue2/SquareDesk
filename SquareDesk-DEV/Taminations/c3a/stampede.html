Stampede

Parts for Centers: 3
Parts for Ends: 2

From a 3/4 Tag or other applicable formations: Centers Trade The Wave, Hinge & Cross (Trailers diagonal Pull By using the outside hand) as the Ends Cross Cast Back and Pass In. A 3/4 Tag ends in an Eight Chain Thru.
