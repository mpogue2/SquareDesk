3/4 Mix

Parts: 3

From a R-H Mini-Wave Box, a Diamond with the Centers in a R-H Mini-Wave, or other applicable formations: Those who can, Right Arm Turn 3/4; in the resulting Line or Wave the centers Cross Run; then the new centers Trade. Ends in a Line or Wave.
