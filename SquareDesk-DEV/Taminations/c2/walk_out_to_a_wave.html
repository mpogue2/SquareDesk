Walk Out to a Wave

From columns: #1 and 2 dancers Trail Off and have the new end Run. #3 dancer Circulate once and U-Turn Back (toward the center), #4 dancer Circulate once and Veer out to become ends. Finishes in parallel waves. This call cannot be fractionalized.
